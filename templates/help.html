How to add my medicine into my calendar?

Click calendar from left side menu buttons and click calendar date you want to add. Type your medicine information you want to follow up according to it's manuel.

May I save my measurements into a log such as blood preasure or sugar level?

Yes, it is possible clicking measurement log item from left side menu.

May I list my completed or continuous medicines?

From left side menu, you can get summary of status of your medicine as a list.
